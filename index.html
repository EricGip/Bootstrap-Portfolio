About Me

Currently completing my fourth and final year at UC Davis with a Statistics major while attending the UC Davis Full-Stack Boot Camp.

I chose my major because I initially had interest in Data Science / AI, but I soon realized I enjoyed the coding aspects more than the analytical aspects after my first Risk Analyst job.

I have been learning Statistical Learning with R from my courseload and self-studying Python in my freetime. The interesting data sets became larger and slower, so I earned an AWS certificate to learn about the cloud and data lakes to improve efficiency.

I needed a way to showcase my projects so I began learning Django, I kept going down the rabbit hole and learned about the whole web development process and began learning JavaScript.

I have a passion for technology, learning, and building things. To fill in my knowledge gaps from my self-studying, I decided to enroll in the UC Davis Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp and fully make the career change.
